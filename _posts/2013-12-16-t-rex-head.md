**Tools & Materials Used**

* Blue Foam
* 1/2" PVC pipes
* Plaster Bandages
* Acrylic Paints
* 3M Spray Adhesive
* 18 Gauge Aluminum Wire
* Hand Sander
* X-Acto Knives
* Hack Saw
* Rhino CAD Software

Time taken : 5 days, 2 people ( 50-60 man hours )
